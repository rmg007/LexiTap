## Status and Scope

LexiTap is a freemium ESL vocabulary mobile app (Expo React Native + on-device SQLite, with a
Supabase cloud-sync mirror). It is offline-first: the large majority of user data lives on the
device; the cloud is a backup/sync mirror. This policy covers the mobile app, the marketing website
([lexitap.app](http://lexitap.app)), and the teacher referral portal.

## Part A — Privacy Policy

### 1. Who We Are

The data controller for LexiTap is `{{LEGAL_ENTITY}}` ("we", "us"), contactable at
`{{CONTROLLER_ADDRESS}}` and `{{PRIVACY_EMAIL}}`. LexiTap is operated by a solo founder; there is no
separately appointed Data Protection Officer (not required at our scale under GDPR Art. 37).

### 2. Data We Collect

We collect only what the app needs to function. Most data is generated and stored on your device.
We do not sell personal data, we do not show third-party ads, and we do not run advertising
trackers.

| Category | Specific data | Where stored | Source |
|----------|---------------|--------------|--------|
| Account | Email, display name, auth provider (email or Google), IANA timezone | Supabase `user_accounts` + device | You / Google Sign-In |
| Authentication | Password hash (email) or Google OAuth token | Supabase Auth (managed) | You / Google |
| Learning progress | SRS state: mastery, review dates, attempt counts (`user_progress`); attempt log (`quiz_attempts`, `quiz_sessions`) | Device SQLite (encrypted blob backup to Supabase Storage, Phase 3+) | Generated by use |
| Purchase history | Purchased tiers, receipt tokens | RevenueCat (external processor) | Apple / Google purchase |
| Gamification | Current/longest streak, total sessions, words mastered, last activity date | Device SQLite (encrypted blob backup to Supabase Storage, Phase 3+) | Generated by use |
| Usage analytics | App-open, lesson start/complete, quiz, conversion events — pseudonymous, aggregated | Device `event_log`; aggregate metrics only leave device | Generated by use |
| Diagnostics | Crash reports, error traces, app/OS version, device model | Error-monitoring provider (§5) | Automatic on crash |
| Teacher (portal only) | Name, email, country, referral code, non-cash reward records | Supabase `teachers`, `referrals` | Teacher signup |

We do not collect: precise location, contacts, photos, microphone/voice recordings (LexiTap does no
pronunciation recording), health data, or government IDs.

### 3. Why We Collect It (Purposes)

- Deliver the core learning experience and spaced-repetition scheduling (on-device).
- Authenticate you and sync progress across devices so a lost phone does not lose your progress.
- Validate purchases and grant/restore Premium subscriptions, B2B seats, or permanent unlocks.
- Maintain streaks and gamification stats.
- Understand aggregate usage (retention, conversion) to improve the app — see
  [ANALYTICS_PLAN.md](./ANALYTICS_PLAN.md).
- Diagnose crashes and fix bugs — see [ERROR_MONITORING_PLAN.md](./ERROR_MONITORING_PLAN.md).
- Operate the teacher advocate program and issue non-cash Premium rewards.

### 4. Legal Bases (EU/UK Users)

- **Contract (Art. 6(1)(b))** — account, progress sync, purchases.
- **Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f))** — crash diagnostics, security, refund/fraud checks, and
  pseudonymous aggregate analytics, balanced against your rights and kept minimal.
- **Consent (Art. 6(1)(a))** — any optional analytics beyond strictly necessary, requested in-app
  and withdrawable in Settings.
- **Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c))** — retention of purchase/tax records.

Full mapping in [GDPR_COPPA_COMPLIANCE.md](./GDPR_COPPA_COMPLIANCE.md).

### 5. Third Parties and Sub-Processors

| Provider | Role | Data shared |
|----------|------|-------------|
| Supabase | Auth + Postgres + Storage | Account, content errors, encrypted user.db backup (Phase 3+) |
| Apple App Store | iOS payments / IAP | Purchase + receipt (Apple is merchant of record) |
| Google Play | Android payments / IAP | Purchase + receipt (Google is merchant of record) |
| Error-monitoring provider (e.g. Sentry) | Crash/error reporting | Diagnostics, pseudonymous user/session id |
| ElevenLabs | Premium TTS audio (build-time only) | None about end users — used in content production, not at runtime |
Note: ElevenLabs is a build-time content tool. Audio files are baked into the app bundle; the
running app never transmits learner data to ElevenLabs. Flagged because it is a common reviewer
misconception.

### 6. Data Retention

- On-device data: kept until you delete it or uninstall the app.
- Cloud account + sync data: kept while your account is active.
- **Account deletion cascades**: deleting your account deletes all cloud copies
  (`user_accounts`, `content_errors`, `user_db_backups`). On-device
  data remains until you uninstall; you may export first.
- Diagnostics: retained per provider default (e.g. ~90 days), then purged.
- Purchase/tax and teacher reward records: retained as legally required (typically up to 7 years) in
  minimized form, even after account deletion.

### 7. Your Rights and How to Exercise Them

Subject to your region (GDPR/UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and similar), you may access, export, correct,
delete, restrict, and object to processing, and withdraw consent.

- **Export** — in-app "Export my data" (machine-readable JSON); cloud-side export on request.
- **Delete** — in-app "Delete account" triggers the cascade in §6.
- **Access / other rights** — email `{{PRIVACY_EMAIL}}`. We respond within 30 days (GDPR), extendable
  by 60 days for complex requests with notice.
- EU/UK users may complain to their supervisory authority.

### 8. Children and Minors

LexiTap is designed for and directed at **adult** ESL learners and is **not directed to children**.
We do not knowingly create accounts for or collect personal data from children under the minimum age
in their jurisdiction (13 in the US under COPPA; up to 16 in parts of the EU under GDPR Art. 8). We
apply a neutral age gate at signup. If we learn we collected data from a child below the applicable
age without verifiable parental consent, we delete it promptly. Detailed approach in
[GDPR_COPPA_COMPLIANCE.md](./GDPR_COPPA_COMPLIANCE.md).

### 9. Security

Passwords are hashed by Supabase Auth; transport is TLS; cloud rows are protected by Supabase
Row-Level Security so a user can read only their own data. Receipts are validated server-side. We
follow least-privilege for service keys.

### 10. International Transfers

Data may be processed outside your country (e.g. Supabase region, US providers). Where required,
transfers rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards.

### 11. Changes to This Policy

We will post changes here and update the effective date; material changes are surfaced in-app.

### 12. Contact

Privacy: `{{PRIVACY_EMAIL}}` — Support: `{{SUPPORT_EMAIL}}`.
