Delete your account

Applies to the LexiTap app (developer: LexiTap)

Delete from inside the app (instant)

  1. Open LexiTap and go to Settings.
  2. Tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm. Deletion runs immediately.

What gets deleted

  • Your account (email and sign-in identity).
  • Your encrypted progress backups stored in the cloud.
  • The data we hold against your account is deleted immediately and permanently — there is no retention period on our side.

Learning progress stored only on your device stays on your device; delete the app to remove it. Purchase records are managed by the Apple App Store or Google Play (and our entitlement provider, RevenueCat) under their own policies — purchases themselves are not affected, and you can re-download or refund through the store.

Can't access the app?

Email privacy@lexitap.app from the address you signed up with and we'll delete the account for you.

See the Privacy Policy for full details on what we store and why.
