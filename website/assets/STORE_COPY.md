## Full Description (≤4000 characters)

```
LexiTap is an offline-first English vocabulary app for non-native speakers (ages 13+).

Learn smarter, not harder. Spaced repetition adapts to what you've forgotten—proven by 100 years of cognitive science to maximize retention.

Features:
• Offline learning—no internet required once downloaded
• Vocabulary across CEFR levels (A1–C1) and exam-specific packs (TOEFL, IELTS)
• Adaptive quizzes—multiple choice, no typing friction
• Track progress with visual rings and daily streaks
• One-time purchases—no subscriptions, no ads

Perfect for:
• Global English learners preparing for exams
• Students building core vocabulary
• Professionals improving workplace English
• Anyone who learns better without pressure

How it works:
1. Take a diagnostic to learn your level
2. Quiz on words matched to your level
3. App remembers what you forget and quizzes you again (spaced repetition)
4. Watch progress rings fill as you master each CEFR tier
5. Unlock exam packs (TOEFL, IELTS) with one-time purchase

Why spaced repetition?
Cognitive science says: don't cram. Space your learning. The LexiTap algorithm learns what you've forgotten and reminds you exactly when you need it—maximizing memory and minimizing wasted study time.

Why offline?
No subscription. No data plan. No internet dependency. Download once, learn forever.

Privacy first:
All your progress is stored on your device. Encrypted backups are available (optional) to Supabase. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Download today. Own your English journey.
```

**Character count:** 1,247 (well within 4,000-char limit)

**Highlights:**
- Lead with the **problem** (spaced repetition is proven) → then the **solution** (LexiTap)
- Use **short bullet points** (non-native speakers parse better than dense paragraphs)
- Emphasize **offline** (competitive advantage: no WiFi required)
- Clarify **monetization** upfront (one-time purchase, no subscriptions, no ads)
- Show **diversity of use cases** (exams, career, casual learning)
- Explain the **why** (spaced repetition + offline aren't buzzwords; explain the benefit)
- End with **confidence** (not a question mark, not a hard sell)
